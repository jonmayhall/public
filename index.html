DMS Integration

High-level reference for how customers, vehicles, appointments, ROs, parts, and history move between myKaarma and each DMS.

Integration Overview

Data moves automatically on a fixed cadence or system trigger.
Data moves when a user or workflow initiates the action in myKaarma.
Data is pulled or pushed when the repair order is created or updated.
Supported by the integration, but not fully live or enabled in all stores.
Functionality is not supported for this integration.
1 Push-Based DMS (myKaarma ‚Üí DMS)

Reynolds

Area Behavior
Customers Customer updates push from myKaarma automatically; customer data is also available as part of RO traffic.
Vehicles Vehicle details are pushed with RO activity; updates flow as part of the same process.
Appointments Appointments push on-demand from myKaarma into Reynolds
(if any appointments are scheduled).
ROs RO information can be sent on-demand or as part of check-in and workflow completion steps.
Parts Invoices Parts invoice data can flow automatically from myKaarma to Reynolds when enabled in the workflow.
Opcodes & History myKaarma respects DMS labor codes/opcodes; RO and F&I history is available via RO activity and nightly processes.

Automate

Area Behavior
Customers Customer data pushes automatically from myKaarma into Automate for supported workflows.
Vehicles Vehicle information is included with RO/customer updates where supported.
Appointments Appointment activity can be reflected in the DMS where configured; not all scenarios are supported.
ROs & Parts No direct RO or parts invoice writeback from myKaarma into Automate; most flows remain DMS-driven.
History / Opcodes Usage is primarily focused on customer and RO presentation in myKaarma; little writeback beyond that.
2 Pull-Based DMS (DMS ‚Üí myKaarma)

CDK

Area Behavior
Customers Customer records pull from CDK every 15 minutes; historical customers can be pulled on-demand.
Vehicles Vehicle data comes with the RO from CDK; updates follow RO traffic.
Appointments Appointments pull every 15 minutes from CDK; myKaarma can also trigger on-demand updates for active days.
ROs ROs pull every 5 minutes; writeback to CDK is on-demand from ServiceCart and Check-In flows.
Parts & Pricing Parts invoices pull every 15 minutes; parts pricing and availability can be refreshed on-demand from ServiceCart.
F & I / History F&I sales history syncs nightly with up to ~2 years of history; RO history is refreshed nightly as well.

Dealertrack / Arkona

Area Behavior
Customers Customers pull every 5 minutes; historical customers are available on-demand.
Vehicles Vehicles can be pulled on-demand and are commonly loaded with RO traffic.
Appointments Appointments pull every 15 minutes; myKaarma can trigger additional updates as needed.
ROs ROs pull every 2 minutes; writeback from ServiceCart and Check-In is on-demand.
Parts & Pricing Parts invoices pull every 15 minutes; ServiceCart can request pricing and availability on-demand.
History RO and customer history are synced nightly; historical RO pulls are available on-demand when configured.

Lightyear / Dealerbuilt

Area Behavior
Customers Customers pull every 5 minutes; historical customers can be accessed where configured.
Vehicles Vehicles come with RO activity; updates are tied to RO refresh cycles.
Appointments Appointments pull every 5 minutes for supported stores.
ROs ROs pull every 2 minutes; writeback behavior for RO information is on-demand from myKaarma.
Parts Parts invoices pull every 5 minutes; pricing may not be fully integrated for all stores.
History Nightly sync for RO history; customer and RO details can be refreshed on-demand if enabled.

PBS

Area Behavior
Customers Customer pull is limited; most customer data flows from active ROs and appointment activity.
Vehicles Vehicle push/pull is focused around RO and appointment flows rather than standalone sync.
Appointments Appointments pull every 5 minutes; push behavior is on-demand from myKaarma where supported.
ROs ROs pull every 2 minutes and write back on-demand from ServiceCart and Check-In.
Parts Parts invoices are not fully pulled for all PBS configurations; special orders may rely on on-demand lookups.
History RO history syncs nightly; customer history pull capability is on-demand for supported stores.

Tekion

Area Behavior
Customers Customers arrive with the RO from Tekion; myKaarma does not run an independent scheduled customer pull.
Vehicles Vehicles also come with the RO; updates follow Tekion RO changes.
Appointments Appointments pull every 5 minutes; push/writeback behavior is on-demand and partially planned.
ROs ROs pull every 5 minutes; writeback and check-in creation features are planned or enabled per store.
Parts & Pricing Parts invoices, availability, and pricing are supported in the integration roadmap and may be live in limited deployments.
History Historical RO and F&I data can be loaded once during deployment; ongoing syncs follow Tekion integration design.
