DMS Integration

How customers, vehicles, appointments, ROs, invoices, pricing, and history flow between myKaarma and each DMS.

Integration Overview

  • Pull: myKaarma pulls data from the DMS on a schedule.
  • Push: myKaarma pushes data into the DMS.
  • Hybrid: Mix of pull and push behavior.
  • Unsupported: Not supported for this DMS today.
DMS Integration Matrix

Reynolds Push

Customers Customer updates push automatically as part of standard workflows.
Vehicles Vehicle information is included with RO and customer pushes.
Appointments Pushes from myKaarma when generated or updated (if appointments are scheduled).
ROs ROs push from ServiceCart, Check-In, or configured workflow triggers.
Parts / Invoices Can push automatically when workflows support parts invoice generation.
History History syncs via RO traffic and labor codes.

Automate Push

Customers Customers push automatically into Automate.
Vehicles Vehicle updates flow with RO traffic.
Appointments Limited appointment update support; details may vary by store.
ROs No RO write-back path from myKaarma.
Parts No parts invoice push supported.
History Primarily presentation only, with limited push traffic.

CDK Pull

Customers Pull every 15 minutes; historical lookup available on-demand.
Vehicles Vehicle information comes with RO records.
Appointments Pull every 15 minutes; myKaarma can request additional refreshes.
ROs Pull every 5 minutes; write-back is on-demand.
Parts & Pricing Invoices pull every 15 minutes; on-demand pricing available in ServiceCart.
History Nightly sync of RO and F&I history (≈2 years).

Dealertrack / Arkona Pull

Customers Pull every 5 minutes; historical requests supported.
Vehicles Vehicle lookup on-demand; also comes with RO data.
Appointments Pull every 15 minutes.
ROs Pull every 2 minutes; write-back is on-demand.
Parts & Pricing Parts invoices pull every 15 minutes.
History Nightly sync for RO and customer history.

Lightyear / Dealerbuilt Pull

Customers Pull every 5 minutes.
Vehicles Loaded with RO activity.
Appointments Pull every 5 minutes.
ROs Pull every 2 minutes; on-demand write-back.
Parts Parts invoices pull every 5 minutes (varies by store).
History Nightly RO history sync; on-demand refresh supported.

PBS Pull

Customers Customer pulls are limited; relies heavily on RO activity.
Vehicles Vehicle sync is primarily RO-related.
Appointments Pull every 5 minutes; some push may vary by workflow.
ROs Pull every 2 minutes; write-back is on-demand.
Parts Parts invoice pulling is not universal.
History Nightly RO history sync.

Tekion Pull

Customers Customers arrive with the RO; no independent customer pull.
Vehicles Vehicle flow is attached to RO updates.
Appointments Pull every 5 minutes; some write-back is planned.
ROs Pull every 5 minutes; write-back enabled per store.
Parts & Pricing Planned and supported on the roadmap.
History Historical RO load is available during deployment.
